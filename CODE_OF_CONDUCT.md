# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Being respectful
- Accepting constructive criticism

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Community leaders are responsible for clarifying standards.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Community leaders will follow these guidelines.
